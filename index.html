How Quilty Helps You Quit

Quilty combines smart tracking, evidence-based reduction plans, and gamification to make quitting vaping achievable and even engaging.

📊

Effortless Tracking

Log puffs with a single tap, a home screen widget, Bluetooth clicker, NFC tag, or even Siri. Multiple ways to track so you always stay on top of your habits.

🎯

Personalized Plans

Start with a baseline assessment, then choose your path: gradual reduction, quit entirely, or track for awareness. Your plan adapts to your pace.

🔥

Streaks & Achievements

Earn points for staying under your daily limit, unlock achievements, and progress through zones on your journey map. Quitting has never been this motivating.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data is encrypted locally on your device. Quilty works fully offline, and you always have the right to export or delete your data.

📈

Insights & Patterns

Detailed analytics reveal when, where, and why you vape most. Understand your triggers to build strategies that actually work.

🎮

Gamified Journey

Progress through seven unique zones from Puff Purgatory to Zenith Summit. Level up, earn trust tiers, and celebrate every milestone along the way.

About Quilty

Quilty is built by Quilty Tech, LLC, a company based in the United States dedicated to helping people take control of their nicotine habits through technology.

We believe quitting should be personal, not one-size-fits-all. That's why Quilty offers multiple paths forward — whether you want to reduce gradually, quit cold turkey, or simply become more aware of your usage. Our approach is grounded in behavioral science and designed to meet you where you are.
